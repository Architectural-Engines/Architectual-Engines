2.1 **Voting Rules**: 
    Locked in Bylaws

2.2 **Amendment Rules**: 
    Locked. Changes allowed only by 100% unanimous vote of directors plus external auditor verification.

2.3 **Human Voting**:
    Every decision requires a 90% supermajority of all human votes.
    Each director has one human vote, exercised independently.

2.4 **AI Advisory Evaluations**:
    Strictly advisory; carry zero formal voting weight.
    Each director may reference one AI advisory evaluation alongside their vote.
    Directors are never required to vote according to AI output.
    Any divergence from AI advice must be logged as a one-sentence public justification in the immutable record.

2.5 **Prompt Governance**:
    Prompts are immutable appendices to the bylaws.
    Pre-written prompts are fixed at founding.

    Changes or additions require 100% director approval plus independent third-party verification confirming:
    Model followed the exact prompt.
    No injection, hallucination, or alteration occurred.
    Model Rotation & Verification:
    Fixed list of approved models included in bylaws.
    Random model selection via a public, verifiable script (GitHub commit + timestamp).
    Board cannot influence model selection.

2.6 **Full Verification Chain (mandatory before any AI advisory is considered)**:
    Exact prompt used logged.
    Raw model output saved permanently.
    Third-party audit verifies adherence and absence of hallucinations.
    All records hashed and published to the public GitHub ledger within 24 hours.
