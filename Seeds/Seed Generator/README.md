**How to basic guide**:

Seed generator **was/is** used to map intial seeds.  Choice of seed is dependent on use case and exploration.  
-Once generated, most stable seeds:[^1d] are recommended at earlier stage: tuning parameters.
  
**note**:
**stable seeds**: resemble a small tight ball, closer to the center of the graph higher stability.
**supplied**:50 examples of seeds of each movement type || [linear](https://github.com/Architectural-Engines/Architectual-Engines/tree/main/Seeds/Linear%20Intial%20Seeds) || [Oscillatory](https://github.com/Architectural-Engines/Architectual-Engines/tree/main/Seeds/Oscillatory%20Initial%20Seeds) || [circular](https://github.com/Architectural-Engines/Architectual-Engines/tree/main/Seeds/Circular%20Intial%20Seeds) || [random walk](https://github.com/Architectural-Engines/Architectual-Engines/tree/main/Seeds/Random%20Walk%20Initial%20Seeds) ||
